# Roles & Responsibilities

---

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this document is to define clear roles and responsibilities within the management system to ensure accountability, ownership, and efficient operation of processes.

This ensures that:
- Everyone understands what is expected of them,
- Decision-making authority is clear,
- Responsibilities are consistently executed.

---

## 2. Scope
This applies to:
- All personnel who perform, support, or manage processes within the organization,
- All permanent/temporary employees, contractors, and relevant third parties.

---

## 3. Key Roles

| Role | Definition / Description |
|------|--------------------------|
| **Top Management / Executive Sponsor** | Provides direction, ensures resources, approves key decisions |
| **Process Owner** | Owns the process and ensures it is effective and compliant |
| **System Owner** | Ensures systems supporting the process are maintained and secure |
| **Auditor** | Performs audits independently of area under review |
| **Risk Owner** | Responsible for assigned risks and mitigation actions |
| **Incident Manager** | Oversees incident handling, escalation, and communication |

---

## 4. Detailed Responsibilities

### A. Top Management

| Responsibility |
|----------------|
| Sets strategic direction and goals |
| Approves policies, budgets, and major changes |
| Reviews system performance (Management Review) |
| Ensures resources are available to support the system |

---

### B. Process Owner

| Responsibility |
|----------------|
| Defines and maintains process documentation |
| Ensures process performance and reporting of KPIs |
| Ensures staff are trained and competent |
| Approves improvements and corrective actions |

---

### C. Risk Owner

| Responsibility |
|----------------|
| Identifies and assesses risks related to their area |
| Defines mitigation and action plans |
| Ensures risks are reviewed and updated regularly |
| Reports high/critical risks to management |

---

### D. Internal Auditor

| Responsibility |
|----------------|
| Conducts audits independently and objectively |
| Documents findings and collects evidence |
| Verifies corrective action effectiveness |
| Reports results to management |

---

### E. Incident Manager

| Responsibility |
|----------------|
| Coordinates the response to incidents |
| Ensures logging, investigation, and documentation |
| Communicates status to stakeholders |
| Leads post-incident review and lessons learned |

---

### F. All Employees

| Responsibility |
|----------------|
| Follow policies and procedures |
| Protect company information and assets |
| Report incidents and risks immediately |
| Participate in required training |

---

## 6. Review and Updates
Roles and responsibilities must be reviewed:

- Annually, or
- When organizational changes occur
