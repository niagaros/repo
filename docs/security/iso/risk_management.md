# Risk Management Procedure & Checklist

---

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of the Risk Management process is to identify, analyze, evaluate, and reduce risks that could impact objectives, operations, security, or compliance.

This procedure ensures risks are managed consistently and transparently.

---

## 2. Scope
This applies to:

- All departments, processes, assets, and projects
- All types of risk (operational, security, compliance, financial, etc.)
- Employees, contractors, and third parties involved in risk-related activities

---

## 3. Risk Management Objectives
Risk Management must ensure:

| Objective | Definition |
|----------|-------------|
| **Identification** | Risks are identified early and proactively |
| **Assessment** | Probability, impact, and severity are evaluated objectively |
| **Treatment** | Risks are reduced, avoided, transferred, or accepted |
| **Monitoring** | Risks are reviewed regularly and updated |

---

## 4. Roles & Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| **Risk Owner** | Owns the risk and is accountable for decisions and actions |
| **Risk Manager / Compliance Lead** | Maintains the risk register and oversees the process |
| **All Employees** | Report risks and follow risk mitigation requirements |
| **Management** | Approves acceptance of medium/high risks and provides resources |

## 5. Risk Ranking Matrix

| Severity | Probability | Impact | Typical Action |
|----------|-------------|--------|----------------|
| **Low** | Low | Low | Monitor |
| **Medium** | Low/Med | Medium | Mitigation recommended |
| **High** | Medium/High | High | Mitigation required |
| **Critical** | High | High | Immediate escalation to management |

---

## 6. Documentation Requirements

The following artifacts must be maintained:

- Risk Register
- Mitigation plans
- Evidence of review and updates


## 7. Review Cycle

Risk reviews must occur:

- At least **quarterly**, and
- During major changes or incidents
