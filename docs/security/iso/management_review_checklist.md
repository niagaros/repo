Use this checklist to perform and document the required ISMS management review.

---

## Management Review Preparation

| Control Point | Check | How to Verify |
|----------------|--------|--------------|
| Has the management review meeting been scheduled? | [ ] | Check meeting invite, agenda, or calendar entry. |
| Is the agenda documented and aligned with ISO 27001 requirements? | [ ] | Compare agenda with ISO 27001 clause ~9.3 management review topics. |
| Have all relevant inputs been collected (audit results, risks, improvements, incidents)? | [ ] | Verify collection in the management review input folder. |
| Are required stakeholders invited (top management, ISMS manager, security officer)? | [ ] | Check attendee list. |

---

## Required Input / Agenda Topics

| Required Input (ISO 27001 Clause 9.3) | Check | How to Verify |
|----------------------------------------|--------|--------------|
| Status of actions from previous management reviews | [ ] | Review previous meeting minutes and action items. |
| Internal audit results | [ ] | Confirm audit report is available. |
| Status of corrective actions and improvements | [ ] | Check corrective action tracker. |
| Changes affecting the ISMS (strategy, organization, technology) | [ ] | Review business planning documents or change logs. |
| Results of risk assessment and risk treatment | [ ] | Validate the risk register and status of treatment plans. |
| Status of security objectives and KPI performance | [ ] | Review KPI dashboard or metrics report. |
| Information security incidents and trends | [ ] | Review incident log and post-incident analysis reports. |
| Opportunities for improvement | [ ] | Evidence in improvement register or lessons learned documents. |

---

## Management Review Execution

| Control Point | Check | How to Verify |
|----------------|--------|--------------|
| The meeting is executed according to the agenda | [ ] | Validate meeting minutes. |
| Discussion and decisions are recorded | [ ] | Check minutes for decisions, not just observations. |
| Evidence of management engagement and leadership | [ ] | Confirm action assignments and decision-making. |

---
