# Risk Assessment 
Risk Assessment ensures that an organization identifies, analyzes, and evaluates risks that could impact the security and reliability of systems and data. This SOC 2 criterion requires a structured approach to recognizing threats, vulnerabilities, and potential business impacts. It includes performing regular risk assessments, maintaining a risk register, prioritizing risks based on severity, and implementing appropriate mitigation measures. Effective risk assessment helps organizations proactively manage security threats and maintain strong operational resilience.


## Requirements  
- [ ] Formal risk assessment completed  
- [ ] Defined threat and vulnerability identification  
- [ ] Risk scoring methodology used  
- [ ] Risk treatment plan documented  
- [ ] Annual review and management approval
