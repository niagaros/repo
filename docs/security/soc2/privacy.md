# Privacy 
Privacy focuses on how an organization collects, uses, stores, discloses, and disposes of personal information. This SOC 2 criterion ensures that personal data is handled according to established privacy principles and legal requirements. It includes transparency with users, obtaining consent when required, limiting data collection, enforcing retention and deletion practices, and protecting personal information from unauthorized access.
