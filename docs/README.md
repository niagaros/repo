# Documentation Overview

Below is a short introduction for each documentation category, describing what users can expect to find. These sections help team members, partners, and customers quickly understand where to look for the right information.

## Internal 

- Internal procedures (DevOps, development, cloud management)
- Team workflows, onboarding instructions, escalation plans
- Architecture diagrams and internal system design
- Technical notes, troubleshooting guides, internal-only strategies
- Internal audit results, reviews, planning documents

These documents are confidential and support daily operations and internal decision-making.

## Overview

- Company mission, product vision, and roadmap
- Explanation of the platform architecture
- High-level diagrams of Niagaros and cloud integrations
- Overview of major modules (dashboards, metrics, access key monitoring)
- Glossary of important concepts

This section gives a complete, non-technical “big picture” of the Niagaros ecosystem.

## Partner

- Company mission, product vision, and roadmap
- Explanation of the platform architecture
- High-level diagrams of Niagaros and cloud integrations
- Overview of major modules (dashboards, metrics, access key monitoring)
- Glossary of important concepts

This section gives a complete, non-technical “big picture” of the Niagaros ecosystem.

## Public

- Public company overview and mission
- Marketing materials, product features, pricing documentation
- Public FAQs, onboarding guides, and user manuals
- Compliance summaries and non-confidential policies

This documentation can be safely shared externally and represents Niagaros to customers.

## Security

- ISO 27001 policies (Risk, Access Control, Incident, Audit)
- Statement of Applicability (SoA)
- Risk Register and Risk Treatment Plans
- Cloud configurations (AWS IAM, KMS, S3, CloudTrail, etc.)
- Incident response playbooks
- Security architecture diagrams
- Data classification and retention guidelines

This section contains the essential documents required for audits, compliance checks, and internal security management.
